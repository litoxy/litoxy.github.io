#### With Powershell:

##### Open Powershell as administrator
Launch command:

```perl
 & 'C:\Program Files\Docker\Docker\DockerCli.exe' -SwitchDaemon
```


OR, with cmd:

##### Open cmd as administrator
Launch command:

```perl
 "C:\Program Files\Docker\Docker\DockerCli.exe" -SwitchDaemon
```
